What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It's the practice of optimising your website to appear in AI-generated answers from systems like ChatGPT, Google's AI Overview, Bing Copilot, and voice assistants.

GEO vs SEO: What's the Difference?

Traditional SEO GEO
Optimise for search result rankings Optimise for AI-generated answers
Focus on keywords Focus on clear, quotable statements
Get users to click your link Get AI to cite your content
Compete for 10 blue links Compete to be the source AI references

SEO and GEO aren't opposites. Good SEO is the foundation for good GEO. But GEO adds new considerations.

How AI Search Works

When someone asks ChatGPT "What's the best way to unclog a drain?", the AI:

  1. Searches its knowledge for relevant information
  2. Looks for authoritative sources with clear answers
  3. Synthesises a response, potentially citing sources
  4. Delivers a direct answer, not a list of links

To be included in that answer, your content needs to be:

  • Clear - Direct statements, not vague marketing
  • Structured - Easy for AI to extract key points
  • Authoritative - Demonstrates expertise
  • Quotable - Contains specific, citable information

GEO Best Practices

1. Write Direct Answers

Instead of burying information in marketing language, lead with clear statements:

Weak: "Our team of dedicated professionals brings years of experience to every plumbing challenge, ensuring quality results."

Strong: "Emergency plumbing services available 24/7 in Cape Town. Average response time: 45 minutes. Services include burst pipes, blocked drains, and geyser repairs."

2. Use FAQ Format

AI systems love question-and-answer formats. They're easy to extract and cite:

<h3>How much does a plumber cost in Cape Town?</h3>
<p>Call-out fees typically range from R350-R500.
Hourly rates are R450-R650 for standard work,
with emergency rates 50% higher.</p>

3. Include Specific Data

AI systems prefer specific, factual information over generalities:

  • Prices and price ranges
  • Service areas
  • Response times
  • Qualifications and certifications

4. Add FAQPage Schema

Mark up your FAQ content with structured data so AI systems can easily identify questions and answers:

{
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [{
    "@type": "Question",
    "name": "How much does a plumber cost?",
    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "Call-out fees range from R350-R500..."
    }
  }]
}
